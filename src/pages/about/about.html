Accurately Track Downtime

Downtime is often associated with just equipment failures, however any unplanned event that causes your line to stop is downtime and knowing the exact reasons in real-time is key to keeping the line producing.

Industrial Intelligence has created a way to help you determine your TRUE downtime and make sure you fully understand WHY giving you actionable intelligence to increase productivity and Overall Equipment Efficiency (OEE).
