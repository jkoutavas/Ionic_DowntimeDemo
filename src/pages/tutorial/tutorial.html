Welcome to the Industrial Intelligence downtime demo app


Our downtime demo app gives a practical preview of our IIoT-based downtime tracking services in action.

What is downtime tracking?


Downtime is often associated with just equipment failures, however any unplanned event that causes your line to stop is downtime and knowing the exact reasons in real-time is key to keeping the line producing.

What's in this demo app?


We've pulled some data from Pacesetter Steel's three Service Centers and populated each site with some steel blanking machines referred to as "Red Buds". We then give you the tools to quickly summize the current state of the system, from machine level, to service center level, all the way out to the company as a whole.
